Dinesh's food truck



Masala Dosa

Dinesh's masala dosa is known all over bengaluru. Unlike other place Dinesh's
food truck adds an extra bit of butter on your dosa.They also give you unlimited
chutney and sambar refills.








Pani puri

Like all the other food items present at the food truck the, pani puri is also popular
among the locals.Dinesh also sells it for cheap and with good quality.One plate
of pani puri contains 7 puris which no other shops offer.They also give upto to
2 free refills of the pani(water).

Biryani

Finally the most famous in the area is Dinesh's biryani which according to
the locals is the most delicious briyani they have eaten.Unlike every other
meal you get something along with it which you can choose between khubz
and kebabs.





Chai

Along with all those foods Dinesh's food truck also offers you traditional chai
(Indian tea) which after a long day can be refreshing.







Masala chai

You might be tired of traditional chai well Dinesh decided to mix it up and make
it into masala chai.
